Your Privacy Matters

At Food Fusion, we’re committed to protecting your data and ensuring a safe, delightful experience.

Introduction

We value your trust. This Privacy Policy explains how Food Fusion collects, uses, and safeguards your information when you use our website and services.

What We Collect

To provide a seamless experience, we collect:

  • Personal Details: Name, email, phone number, and delivery address for orders and accounts.
  • Usage Data: Information on how you interact with our site, like searches and clicks.
  • Payment Info: Handled securely by third-party gateways (we don’t store this data).

How We Use Your Data

Your information helps us:

  • Process and deliver your orders efficiently.
  • Enhance our website’s functionality.
  • Send personalized offers (with your consent).
  • Respond to your inquiries promptly.

Data Sharing

We may share your data with:

  • Local food centers to fulfill your orders.
  • Trusted third-party providers for payments and analytics.
  • Legal authorities if required by law.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and update your personal information.
  • Opt out of marketing communications.
  • Request data deletion, subject to legal obligations.

Contact us at support@foodfusion.com.

Data Security

We use encryption and secure servers to protect your data. While we strive for maximum security, no online system is 100% risk-free.

Policy Updates

We may update this policy periodically. Changes will be posted here.

Effective Date: August 5, 2025
