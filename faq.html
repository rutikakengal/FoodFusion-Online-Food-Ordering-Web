Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. How do I place an order?

Ans. Browse our menu, add your favorite items to the cart, and proceed to checkout. Follow the on-screen instructions to complete your order.

Q2. What payment methods are accepted?

Ans. We accept major credit/debit cards, UPI, and cash on delivery for your convenience.

Q3. Can I cancel or modify my order?

Ans. Yes, you can cancel or modify your order before it is prepared. Please contact our support team as soon as possible for assistance.

Q4. How long does delivery usually take?

Ans. Delivery typically takes 30–45 minutes, depending on your location and order volume.

Q5. Do you deliver in all areas?

Ans. We deliver to most areas within the city. Enter your address at checkout to confirm delivery availability.
